Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings (gear icon) and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any active subscriptions or lifetime purchases linked to your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings > your name > Subscriptions > Soulwise, then tap Cancel.

How do I change my daily reminder time?

Go to Settings > Reminders. You can set separate morning and evening reminder times with the time picker.

Can I use Soulwise offline?

Yes! All 855 affirmations, breathing exercises, and features work completely offline. No internet connection required.

How does mood matching work?

Each affirmation has mood weights for 7 different moods. When you select your mood, Soulwise creates a personalized set: 70% mood-matched affirmations and 30% discovery affirmations, refreshed daily.

How do I write my own affirmations?

Go to the Favorites tab and tap the + button in the top right. Type your affirmation and save it. Your custom affirmations appear in a dedicated "My Affirmations" section.

How do I delete my data?

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all data completely. We never store anything on external servers.
