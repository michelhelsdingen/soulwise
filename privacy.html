Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 30, 2026

Soulwise: Mind & Soul ("Soulwise", "we", "our") is developed by Michel Helsdingen. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data Soulwise collects, how it is used, and your rights.

TL;DR: Soulwise does not collect personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

Soulwise does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Soulwise stores your preferences and usage data locally on your device using Apple's standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults). This includes:

  • Your selected mood and category preferences
  • Favorite affirmations
  • Custom affirmations you write
  • Daily streak count
  • Daily intention text
  • Mood history entries
  • Voice and reminder settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Analytics

Soulwise may use TelemetryDeck for anonymous, privacy-first analytics. TelemetryDeck does not collect personal data, does not use cookies, and does not track users across apps. All data is aggregated and anonymous. You cannot be identified from this data.

4. In-App Purchases

Soulwise uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase transactions through Apple's App Store. RevenueCat may collect anonymous transaction data as described in their privacy policy. We do not have access to your Apple ID, payment information, or billing details.

5. Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, Soulwise schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any external servers.

6. Third-Party Services

Soulwise integrates the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — In-app purchase management (privacy policy)
  • TelemetryDeck — Anonymous analytics (privacy policy)

Neither service collects personally identifiable information through Soulwise.

7. Children's Privacy

Soulwise is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app. We have no data to delete on our end because we never receive it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
